Ingredients

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the packaging and let it cool completely.
  2. Stir garlic and dried herbs in a heated pan for 30 seconds. Mix in tomatoes, break up larger pieces.
  3. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add balsammic vinegar and basil.
